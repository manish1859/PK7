Welcome To PK7 Education of Education

PK7 Education is a dedicated learning center in Kotputli, offering quality education from Class 1st to 12th. We provide all subjects for Classes 1st to 8th, and subject-specific coaching like English, Science, SST, and Math for Classes 9th & 10th, along with PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Math) for senior secondary students.

Apart from academics, we focus on overall development through various computer courses such as RS-CIT, CCC, and Basic Computer, and also offer extra activities like Dance, Judo, and Karate to build confidence, discipline, and creativity in students.

Meet Our Founder

Krisan Kumar Saini is the visionary founder and director of PK7 Education. He completed his 12th class in 2019, followed by a one-year Accounting course. With a strong academic background, he earned his Bachelor of Arts (B.A.) in 2022 and went on to complete his Master of Arts (M.A.) in 2024.

After completing his graduation, he was inspired to contribute to education in a meaningful way. He started a YouTube channel named PK7, where he began teaching Computer fundamentals, RS-CIT, and content for competitive exams. He also started offering tuition classes for students from Class 1 to 8.

On 17th February 2025, he turned his vision into reality by establishing PK7 Education as a fully functional offline center at Prince Plaza, Kotputli. Under his leadership, PK7 aims to provide affordable and high-quality education to students of all levels.

Today, Krisan Kumar Saini continues to guide PK7 Education with dedication, innovation, and a passion for teaching.
